About

Khaing Hsu Wai

ခိုင်ဆုဝေ

Hello! I am Khaing Hsu Wai (カイン), a silly but hardworking Ph.D candidate at Okayama University under Funabiki Lab.
I am also a member of Language Understanding (LU) Lab under Prof. Ye.
Currently, my research is focused on Programming Learning Assistant System to support the programming education for the novice students and
creative text generation for low-resource languages.

My research interests focus on Educational Technology, Machine Learning, and Natural Language Processing.
You can have a look at my research publication in here.
If you are interested in discussing research collaborations or need some guidance, please feel free to contact me. I am always ready to help.

I like to do volunteer works to support every community. I also like traveling and seeking new adventures. One big dream of mine is to see the northern lights!

My life quote is "Be Free. Be True. Be You."!
